# Timeline & Pricing

**RFP #MC-2026-0417 — Meridian Components Inventory Dashboard Modernization**

---

## Timeline

Proposed start: **May 19, 2026** (one week after award, assuming selection by May 12).
Total duration: **10 weeks**.

| Phase | Weeks | Deliverables |
|---|---|---|
| **Phase 1 — Onboarding & Stabilization** | 1–2 | Architecture doc (R4), test framework setup, first browser tests live (R3 baseline) |
| **Phase 2 — Reports Remediation** | 3–5 | All R1 defects resolved, Reports fully covered by regression tests |
| **Phase 3 — Restocking Feature** | 6–9 | R2 backend endpoint + frontend view, Playwright tests for Restocking flow |
| **Phase 4 — Close & Dark Mode** | 10 | D3 dark mode, full test suite review, handoff documentation, stakeholder walkthrough |

**Checkpoints.** End-of-phase reviews with Meridian IT and operations team. Go/no-go before each phase transition.

**Milestones.**

- Week 2: Architecture doc delivered to IT; test framework running
- Week 5: Reports remediation complete; IT approves R1 closure
- Week 9: Restocking view in UAT with operations team
- Week 10: All deliverables accepted; handoff complete

**Risk.** The primary schedule risk is scope discovery in R1 — if the Reports defect count significantly exceeds eight, we will flag it at the Week 3 checkpoint and agree on prioritization with Meridian before proceeding.
