**Outcome:** IT team approved ongoing deployments within 6 weeks of engagement start. Defect backlog cleared in full.

## Engagement 2 — Restocking & Procurement Feature, Manufacturing Client

**Client:** Contract manufacturer, APAC operations (confidential)
**Year:** 2024
**Scope:** Designed and built a purchase order recommendation engine integrated into an existing inventory system. Inputs: stock levels, demand forecasts, configurable budget ceiling. Output: ranked reorder recommendations with justification visible to ops staff.
**Outcome:** Operations team reduced manual reorder analysis time by approximately 60%. Feature adopted by three warehouse locations within two months of launch.
**Relevance:** Direct parallel to R2. Same feature type, same design principle (transparent algorithm, ops-readable output).

## Engagement 3 — Multi-locale UI Modernization, Distribution Company

**Client:** European logistics firm with operations in 6 countries (confidential)
**Year:** 2023
**Scope:** Refreshed a legacy operations dashboard to align with updated brand guidelines. Extended i18n to four new locales. Implemented dark mode for warehouse floor stations operating in low-light environments.
**Outcome:** Tokyo and Frankfurt offices adopted localized views within 4 weeks of rollout. Zero regression incidents post-launch.
**Relevance:** Direct parallel to D1, D2, and D3. Covers brand-aligned UI refresh, internationalization, and dark mode in a warehouse operations context.
