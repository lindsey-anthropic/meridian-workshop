# Executive Summary

**Prepared for:** Meridian Components, Inc.
**RFP #:** MC-2026-0417
**Submitted by:** Accenture
**Date:** April 27, 2026

---

Meridian Components has built a functional inventory dashboard — but functional is not the same as finished. The previous vendor left the Reports module incomplete, delivered no automated tests, and handed off minimal documentation. Your operations team is working around defects daily, your IT team has blocked further changes for lack of test coverage, and your Tokyo staff are navigating an English-only interface. The system works, but it is not working for you.

We have reviewed the RFP, the vendor handoff notes, and the existing codebase. Our assessment: the required work is well-scoped, the risks are known, and the path forward is straightforward — provided the next vendor comes in with eyes open and a commitment to finishing what was started.

**What we propose to deliver:**

- A fully remediated Reports module, with all documented defects resolved and filter behavior consistent across the application
- A new Restocking view that gives your operations team purchase order recommendations based on live stock levels, demand forecasts, and a configurable budget ceiling
- End-to-end automated browser tests covering all critical filter interactions, giving your IT team the coverage they need to approve ongoing changes
- A current-state architecture document suitable for internal handoff — something the previous vendor never produced

We will also request your brand guidelines in week one to scope a UI refresh, and will include internationalization and dark mode as add-on options once the required work is complete.

**Our commitment to Meridian is simple:** every deliverable in this proposal will be accompanied by working code, passing tests, and documentation your team can rely on going forward.

We are ready to begin on contract execution and can deliver the full required scope within twelve weeks.
