# Executive Summary

**Proposal for:** Meridian Components, Inc.
**RFP:** MC-2026-0417
**Submitted by:** Accenture
**Date:** April 28, 2026

---

Meridian Components has a working inventory dashboard and a team that relies on it daily — but the previous engagement ended before the work was finished. Reports are broken, the Tokyo warehouse staff are working around an English-only interface, there are no automated tests, and a new capability the operations team has been waiting on (restocking recommendations) was never started. Your IT team has, reasonably, blocked changes until there's test coverage to back them up.

We propose to complete and extend what was started. Our approach is to resolve the outstanding defects first (R1), establish the automated test foundation that unblocks future changes (R3), then deliver the Restocking view your operations team needs (R2) — all within a documented architecture your IT team can maintain going forward (R4). UI modernization, full i18n, and dark mode are scoped as follow-on work within the same engagement if timeline permits.

This is not a rebuild. The existing Vue/Python stack is sound and the codebase is in scope for incremental improvement. We'll work directly in the code the previous vendor delivered, verify our understanding against the running system, and document what we find — including anything the handoff notes missed.

We understand that timeline predictability matters as much as price. Our proposal includes a phased delivery plan with clear milestones so Meridian has visibility at each stage, not just at the end.

**Proposed fee:** $75,000 fixed (see Pricing section for assumptions and T&M alternative).
