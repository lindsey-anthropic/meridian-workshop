# Executive Summary

**RFP #MC-2026-0417 — Inventory Dashboard Modernization**
**Response by: Accenture | Due: May 8, 2026**

---

Meridian Components has built a solid operational foundation — three warehouses, a growing APAC footprint, a capable team. The dashboard meant to support that work has not kept pace. The previous vendor delivered an incomplete system: no automated tests, unresolved defects in the Reports module, and documentation thin enough to be almost useless. R. Tanaka and her team deserve a tool that works — not another round of promises.

Our approach is straightforward: fix what is broken first, then build what is missing.

**Phase 1 (May–June)** — Audit and remediate all defects in the Reports module; establish automated test coverage. Tests are the condition for everything else: without them, IT is right to block changes, and every new feature is a risk. We deliver them first, not last.

**Phase 2 (June–August)** — Build the Restocking view: purchase order recommendations based on current stock levels, demand forecast, and an operator-supplied budget ceiling. This is Tanaka's direct ask — one place, one decision, clear logic.

**Phase 3 (August–September)** — Architecture documentation, plus desired items (UI refresh, i18n for Tokyo, dark mode) in priority order agreed with the client.

We know this class of system. We also understand that the problem is not purely technical — Meridian needs confidence that the next vendor will finish the job. We offer fixed-fee pricing with a not-to-exceed cap and weekly check-ins with Tanaka so there are no surprises at delivery.

Target delivery: all in-scope items completed by **September 30, 2026**.
