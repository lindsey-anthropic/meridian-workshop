# Pricing

**Proposal in response to RFP #MC-2026-0417**

---

## Fee Structure

We propose a fixed-fee engagement model, providing Meridian with full cost certainty across the delivery period. All required deliverables (R1–R4) are covered under the base engagement fee. No time-and-materials exposure; no change orders for scope items enumerated in this proposal.

---

## Base Engagement Fee

| Component | Fee |
|---|---|
| R1 — Reports Module Remediation | $18,000 |
| R2 — Restocking Recommendations | $42,000 |
| R3 — Automated Browser Testing | $22,000 |
| R4 — Architecture Documentation | $8,000 |
| Project Management & Delivery Oversight | $10,000 |
| **Total Fixed Fee** | **$100,000** |

---

## Early-Completion Incentive

As outlined in our executive summary, we are sufficiently confident in our delivery approach to offer a performance-based incentive structure. Should all required deliverables (R1–R4) receive formal client acceptance on or before **July 17, 2026**, an early-completion bonus of **$35,000** will become payable, bringing the total engagement value to **$135,000**.

This structure reflects our commitment to timeline predictability — we do not offer incentives we do not intend to earn.

| Scenario | Total Engagement Value |
|---|---|
| Standard completion (by July 24, 2026) | $100,000 |
| Early completion (by July 17, 2026) | $135,000 |

---

## Desired Items (D1–D3)

Desired items are not included in the base engagement fee. Should Meridian elect to authorize any or all of the following, they will be delivered under a separate fixed-fee addendum at the rates below:

| Item | Estimated Fee |
|---|---|
| D1 — UI Modernization | $12,000 |
| D2 — Internationalization Expansion | $6,000 |
| D3 — Dark Mode | $5,000 |
| **All three (D1–D3 bundle)** | **$19,000** |

Desired items may be authorized individually or as a bundle. Bundle pricing reflects a 17% reduction versus individual authorization.

---

## Assumptions & Exclusions

1. **Infrastructure:** No cloud hosting, database provisioning, or DevOps pipeline setup is included in scope. The existing local deployment model is retained.
2. **Licensing:** No third-party software licenses are required. All proposed tooling (Vue 3, FastAPI, Playwright) is open-source.
3. **Travel:** All work is performed remotely. No travel costs are anticipated or included.
4. **Change management:** Scope changes requested by Meridian after contract execution will be assessed and quoted separately. Changes to the desired items (D1–D3) after authorization are subject to the same process.
5. **Validity:** This pricing is valid for 30 days from the proposal submission date of April 24, 2026.
