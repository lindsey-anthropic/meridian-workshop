## Assumptions

This fixed fee is based on the following assumptions. Material changes to any of these may require a change order.

1. **R1 scope:** Defect count is consistent with the previous vendor's characterization ("at least eight issues"). If the audit reveals a materially larger defect set (>15 distinct issues), we will flag this before proceeding and discuss options with Meridian.
2. **R2 scope:** Restocking recommendations operate on existing in-memory JSON data. No ERP integration, supplier API, or database migration is included.
3. **R3 scope:** Four flows as specified. Additional flows are out of scope for this fee.
4. **Access:** Meridian provides timely access to R. Tanaka and one IT contact for questions and milestone reviews. Delays caused by Meridian's availability are not counted against the project timeline.
5. **Desired items (D1–D3):** Included as stretch at no additional cost. Not guaranteed.

---

## Pricing Basis

We are quoting fixed-fee rather than T&M because Meridian's RFP signals a preference for predictability (no budget range disclosed, timeline weighted at 20% of evaluation). A fixed fee transfers schedule risk to us — which we accept given our familiarity with Vue/FastAPI stacks of this type.
