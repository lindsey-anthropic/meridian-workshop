# Pricing

**RFP MC-2026-0417 — Inventory Dashboard Modernization**

---

We are proposing a **fixed-fee engagement** for the required scope (R1–R4). Fixed-fee gives Meridian cost predictability and eliminates the risk of runaway T&M billing — which aligns with what we heard from procurement as a priority.

---

## Required Scope (R1–R4) — Fixed Fee

| Phase | Scope | Fee |
|---|---|---|
| Phase 1 — Foundation (Weeks 1–2) | Architecture review, test suite foundation, Reports audit | $18,000 |
| Phase 2 — Remediation (Weeks 3–4) | Reports defect remediation, extended test coverage | $16,000 |
| Phase 3 — New Capability (Weeks 5–8) | Restocking view, full test suite, handoff | $28,000 |
| **Total** | | **$62,000** |

Not-to-exceed: **$62,000**. No overages without written change order approved by Meridian procurement.

---

## Desired Scope (D1–D3) — Separate Estimate

Desired items are priced separately and would follow the required engagement as a distinct statement of work.

| Item | Estimated Fee |
|---|---|
| D1 — UI Modernization | $12,000 |
| D2 — Internationalization | $8,000 |
| D3 — Dark Mode | $6,000 |
| **D1–D3 Bundle** | **$22,000** |

These estimates assume the required scope has been delivered first. Bundling D1–D3 together reduces coordination overhead.

---

## Assumptions

- Pricing assumes two senior engineers, eight weeks, approximately 20 billable hours per week per person.
- Client provides timely feedback (48-hour turnaround) on the defect list sign-off in Phase 1. Delays that extend the engagement beyond eight weeks will be addressed via written change order.
- Travel is not included. All work performed remotely unless otherwise agreed.
- Any material change in scope (additional requirements, architectural constraints discovered during onboarding) will be raised immediately with a proposed resolution before impacting cost.
