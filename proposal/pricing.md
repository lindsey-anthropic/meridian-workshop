# Pricing

**RFP #MC-2026-0417 — Inventory Dashboard Modernization**

---

We are proposing **time and materials with a not-to-exceed cap** for Phase 1 (required items). This model reflects the reality of the engagement: the previous vendor's limited handoff means some discovery is inevitable, and a fixed-fee bid would require us to pad estimates to absorb that risk — which ultimately costs Meridian more. A NTE cap gives you a firm ceiling with no surprises, while allowing us to deliver efficiently when the work goes smoothly.

## What's Included

- All source code, tests, and documentation delivered to Meridian's repository
- Architecture documentation (R4) — self-contained HTML, no licensing required
- Playwright test suite (R3) — checked into the repository, runnable without additional tooling
- One IT handoff session (up to 2 hours) included in Phase 4

## What's Not Included

- Hosting, infrastructure, or deployment
- Ongoing support or maintenance beyond the engagement
- Procurement integrations or approvals workflow for R2
- Any work outside the logged defect list for R1 (will be flagged and separately authorized)
