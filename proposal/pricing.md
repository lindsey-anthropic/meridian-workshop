# Pricing

**RFP #MC-2026-0417 — Meridian Components**

---

## Model: Fixed-Fee with Defined Scope

We propose a fixed-fee engagement. Meridian bears no exposure to hours overruns on our side; we bear the risk of scoping accurately. This model reflects our confidence in the estimate and removes timeline uncertainty for your procurement process.

---

## Fee Schedule

| Phase | Scope | Weeks | Fixed Fee |
|---|---|---|---|
| Phase 1 — Foundation | Discovery, defect inventory, test harness (R3 baseline) | 1–2 | $8,500 |
| Phase 2 — Remediation | Reports remediation (R1), architecture doc (R4) | 3–4 | $12,000 |
| Phase 3 — New Feature | Restocking view (R2), full test coverage | 5–7 | $18,000 |
| Phase 4 — Desired Items | i18n (D2), dark mode (D3), UI refresh (D1), handoff | 8–10 | $11,500 |
| **Total** | **All required + desired items** | **10 weeks** | **$50,000** |

---

## Payment Terms

| Milestone | Amount |
|---|---|
| Contract signature | 25% ($12,500) |
| Phase 2 complete — Reports remediated, IT sign-off received | 35% ($17,500) |
| Final delivery — all phases complete, handoff session done | 40% ($20,000) |

---

## What's Included

- All work described in the Technical Approach document
- Unlimited revision cycles within defined scope
- Handoff session with Meridian IT (up to 2 hours)
- 30-day warranty period: defects in delivered work fixed at no additional charge

## What's Not Included

- Database migration or infrastructure changes
- Mobile application development
- Integration with external ERP or ordering systems
- Ongoing support or maintenance after warranty period (available under separate retainer)

---

## Assumptions

1. Meridian provides codebase access and a working local development environment within 3 business days of contract signature.
2. A single point of contact on the Meridian side is available for weekly check-ins and the Week 5 restocking logic review.
3. D1 (UI modernization) scope is confirmed no later than end of Phase 2. Significant expansion beyond a visual refresh is a change order.
4. All Reports defects discovered during the Phase 1 audit are covered under Phase 2 — no change order will be issued regardless of how many issues are found.
