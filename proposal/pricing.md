# 5. Pricing

*Accenture response to RFP MC-2026-0417 — Meridian Components, Inc.*

---

## Fixed-Fee Total

**USD $325,000**, inclusive of discovery, parallel delivery, handover, and the desired-capabilities scope (D1, D2, D3) as specified in §3.6 of the technical approach.

---

## Structure

A single fixed-fee envelope. Discovery is included in the fee and not billed separately. The fee covers:

- Phase 1 — Discovery (week 1)
- Phase 2 — Parallel delivery (weeks 2–7) for R1–R4 and D1–D3
- Phase 3 — Handover (week 8) including R4 architecture documentation, knowledge transfer, and engagement closeout

Travel and incidentals — if any are required for on-site sessions — are passed through at cost with prior written approval from Meridian.

---

## Discovery Calibration Clause

If discovery week 1 produces a Statement of Work that materially diverges from the v1 algorithm and scope laid out in §3.2 — specifically the election of cross-warehouse rebalancing or the inclusion of any phase-2 capabilities — Accenture and Meridian execute a single change order before week 2 begins. The change order specifies any additional fee, scope expansion, and timeline impact in writing.

No incremental change orders are issued thereafter, except by mutual written agreement on a separate scope. This structure is intentional: one calibration point at end of discovery, not an open-ended adjustment cycle.

Invoices are issued at each trigger. Payment terms: net 30.

---

## Inclusions

The fixed fee includes:

- All Accenture personnel time (engagement manager, tech lead, engineers, design contribution)
- Project management, status reporting, and weekly Meridian-facing review sessions
- Tooling and infrastructure provided by Accenture during the engagement (CI runners, Playwright environment, internal collaboration tooling)
- Final R4 architecture documentation as a self-contained HTML deliverable
- One half-day knowledge-transfer session with Meridian IT at handover

---

## Exclusions

The fixed fee does not include:

- Cross-warehouse rebalancing logic (handled via the Discovery Calibration Clause if elected)
- A full design-system rebuild beyond the light refresh specified in §3.6 D1
- Production hosting, third-party SaaS tooling licenses, or infrastructure costs Meridian incurs to run the application
- Any work scope arising after engagement closeout — these would be addressed through a separate follow-on engagement

---

## Validity

This pricing is valid for 60 days from the proposal submission date. Renewal of the offer beyond that window is subject to Accenture commercial review.
