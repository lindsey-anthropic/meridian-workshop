# Pricing

**RFP #:** MC-2026-0417
**Prepared by:** Accenture
**Date:** April 27, 2026

---

We are proposing a **fixed-fee engagement** for the required scope (R1–R4), with desired items (D1–D3) priced as optional add-ons. Fixed-fee pricing gives Meridian cost certainty; our assumptions are stated explicitly so any scope change is easy to identify and price fairly.

---

## Required Scope (R1–R4)

| Item | Description | Fee |
|------|-------------|-----|
| R4 | Architecture documentation | $8,000 |
| R1 | Reports module remediation (up to 8 defects) | $22,000 |
| R3 | Automated browser test suite (all filter interactions) | $18,000 |
| R2 | Restocking recommendations view | $32,000 |
| **Total — Required** | | **$80,000** |

---

## Desired Scope (D1–D3) — Optional Add-ons

| Item | Description | Fee |
|------|-------------|-----|
| D1 | UI modernization (brand-aligned, contingent on guidelines) | $15,000 |
| D2 | Internationalization — remaining modules, Japanese locale | $12,000 |
| D3 | Dark mode | $8,000 |
| **Total — Desired (all three)** | | **$35,000** |

---

## Not-to-Exceed

| Scenario | Total |
|----------|-------|
| Required scope only (R1–R4) | $80,000 |
| Required + all desired (R1–R4 + D1–D3) | $115,000 |

Both figures are fixed not-to-exceed. We will not invoice above these amounts without a written change order agreed by both parties.

---

## Payment Schedule

| Milestone | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Contract execution | 25% ($20,000) |
| R4 + R1 delivered and accepted (end of week 5) | 35% ($28,000) |
| R2 + R3 delivered and accepted (end of week 8) | 40% ($32,000) |

Desired items (D1–D3) invoiced separately per item on delivery and acceptance.

---

## Assumptions & Exclusions

- Pricing assumes remote engagement. On-site travel, if requested, billed at cost with prior approval.
- R1 pricing covers up to 8 defects. Additional defects discovered beyond the confirmed list will be quoted separately.
- D1 pricing assumes receipt of Meridian brand guidelines by end of week 1. Delay may affect schedule but not fee.
- No infrastructure, hosting, or licensing costs are included — existing environment assumed.
- Change orders for scope additions require written agreement before work begins.
