# Pricing

**RFP #MC-2026-0417 — Meridian Components Inventory Dashboard Modernization**
**Submitted by:** Accenture&CT Consulting

---

## Pricing Model

We are proposing a **fixed-fee engagement** for the required scope (R1–R4), with optional fixed-fee extensions for desired items (D1–D3) and transactional procurement integration.

Fixed-fee pricing gives Meridian full cost certainty. There are no hourly overruns, no change orders for scope already defined. If we surface more than eight Reports defects during audit, we resolve them — that risk is ours, not Meridian's.

## Assumptions

- Fixed fee covers all required items as scoped in the Technical Approach. Scope changes requested by Meridian after contract signature are handled via a lightweight change order process — we commit to a written estimate within 48 hours of any scope change request.
- Travel is not anticipated. If on-site visits are required, reasonable travel expenses are billed at cost with prior approval.
- Optional extensions are priced independently and can be added or removed at kickoff or at Phase 4 completion without affecting the base engagement fee.
- Transactional procurement integration fee assumes a standard REST API on Meridian's procurement system. Non-standard integration may require re-scoping.
