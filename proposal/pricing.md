# Pricing

**RFP #MC-2026-0417 — Meridian Components Inventory Dashboard Modernization**

---

We are proposing a **time-and-materials engagement with a not-to-exceed (NTE) ceiling**. Meridian pays for actual hours worked; we are capped at the NTE regardless of overruns on our side. This structure gives Meridian cost predictability without requiring us to pad estimates to absorb risk we haven't yet measured.

---

## Blended Rate

| Role | Rate (USD/hour) |
|------|----------------|
| Senior Engineer / Tech Lead | $185 |
| Engineer | $145 |
| **Blended rate** | **$165** |

The engagement will be staffed with one senior engineer (lead and architecture) and one engineer (implementation and testing). The blended rate above reflects the mix across the eight-week engagement.

---

## Not-to-Exceed Ceiling

| Item | Amount (USD) |
|------|-------------|
| Required items (R1–R4) NTE | $58,000 |
| Desired items — D2 + D3 (i18n + dark mode) | $8,500 |
| Desired items — D1 (UI modernization, scoped separately) | $12,000 |
| **Total NTE (all items)** | **$78,500** |

The NTE for required items includes a ~13% buffer above the point estimate to absorb scope uncertainty in R1 (defect count) and R2 (integration complexity). We will not bill beyond $58,000 for R1–R4 regardless of actual hours.

D1 (UI modernization) is priced separately because it requires a design approval step before implementation begins. If Meridian chooses to include D1, we will initiate that conversation at the start of Phase 5 and scope it in writing before proceeding.

## Notes

- Rates are in USD. If Meridian requires invoicing in GBP or JPY for the London or Tokyo entities, we can accommodate with a fixed exchange rate locked at contract execution.
- Travel is not anticipated; all work is remote. If on-site sessions are requested, travel costs are billed at cost and are not included in the NTE.
- This estimate is valid for 30 days from submission (through June 8, 2026).
